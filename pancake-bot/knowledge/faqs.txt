Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are your business hours?
A: We are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. You can still send us messages outside of business hours and we will respond first thing the next business day.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's tracking page. You can also log into your account on our website and check the order status under "My Orders." If you haven't received a tracking number within 48 hours of placing your order, please contact our support team.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Customs duties and import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient. Shipping rates vary by region and will be calculated at checkout.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team through live chat on our website, by emailing support@example.com, or by calling our hotline at +1-800-555-0199. Our live chat agents are available during business hours. Email inquiries are typically answered within 24 hours.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that window, your order enters processing and can no longer be changed. To request a modification or cancellation, please contact our support team immediately with your order number. If the order has already shipped, you will need to follow our return process once you receive the item.
